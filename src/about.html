About Fixera - Handyman in Copenhagen

Learn more about Greg and Fixera: offering handyman services, home maintenance, repairs and installations in Copenhagen with tidy and reliable work.

I’m Greg, the person behind Fixera. I help with small-to-medium home jobs in Copenhagen and nearby areas: repairs, installations, furniture assembly, painting touch-ups, and practical handyman work that needs care and good judgment.

Fixera is built for everyday jobs in real homes and apartments: clear communication, realistic scope, tidy execution, and a finish that feels right in a lived-in space.

  • Clear timing and pricing before work starts
  • Tidy work in occupied homes and apartments
  • Follow-up if something needs attention afterwards

What Fixera is best at

Best fit for practical home jobs that need good judgment and a tidy finish: installations, repairs, furniture assembly, touch-up painting, and the small-to-medium tasks people usually postpone.

How I work

You send photos and a short description. I confirm scope, timing, and price range, arrive with the right tools, work carefully in the space, and leave everything neat when the job is done.

What I do not take

No gas work, no high-voltage rewiring, no major plumbing, no structural work, no roof work, and no mold remediation. If a licensed trade or specialist is the right fit, I say so early.

Frequently Asked Questions

Booking and fit

How do I book a job?

Send photos and a short description on WhatsApp or through the contact form. I reply with a price range or the next step, depending on the job.

How soon can you come?

Many small jobs in Copenhagen can be scheduled within 24-48 hours, depending on scope and availability.

What area do you cover?

I mainly cover Copenhagen and also work in nearby areas such as Frederiksberg, Nørrebro, Østerbro, Gentofte, Gladsaxe, Hellerup, Søborg, and Bagsværd.

What jobs do you take?

Small-to-medium home jobs: repairs, installations, furniture assembly, painting touch-ups, and safe basics in plumbing and electrics.

Pricing and follow-up

How do you price jobs?

Pricing is based on scope, time, and any parts needed. Small jobs often start around 500-1,200 DKK, and I confirm the range before work starts.

What jobs do you not take?

No gas work, no high-voltage rewiring, no major plumbing, no structural work, no roof work, and no mold remediation.

Do you guarantee your work?

Yes. If something needs attention after the job, I come back and sort it out.

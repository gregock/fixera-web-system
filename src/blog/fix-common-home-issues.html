How to Fix Common Home Issues in Copenhagen Apartments

29 October 2025 • Read time: 6 minutes

Table of contents

Old Copenhagen buildings are beautiful, high ceilings, thick walls, and lots of character. But living in them also means dealing with small but annoying issues that keep coming back: loose shelves, cracked walls, or bathroom leaks. For practical storage solutions, see our shelf installation services.

Here are the most common home issues we fix for clients across Copenhagen, and how you can handle them safely.

1. Cracked or uneven walls

Over time, plaster and drywall (gipsvægge) can develop small cracks due to humidity and seasonal movement.

  • Use a flexible filler for small cracks (< 2 mm).
  • For larger ones, apply a fiber-reinforced wall repair compound.
  • Sand gently and repaint once dry.

Tip: If the same crack reopens, it may be structural movement or poor adhesion. Fixera Services can repair and repaint these so the finish lasts years.

2. Drilling into brick or concrete walls

Many apartments have solid brick or concrete walls. Use masonry bits and proper anchors - not standard gypsum plugs.

  • Hammer drill mode with masonry bits.
  • Plugs rated for brick/concrete.
  • Test depth and load before hanging heavy items.

If the wall chips or cracks while drilling, stop. We can mount shelves, mirrors and curtain rods cleanly with dust control. If you're dealing with wall setups or mounting issues, see our TV mounting service in Copenhagen.

3. Bathroom silicone around showers and sinks

Silicone seals wear faster than most people think. When you see black spots, loose edges, or small gaps, it’s time to reseal.

  1. Cut away the old silicone completely.
  2. Clean with alcohol and let dry.
  3. Apply new bathroom-grade silicone in one smooth pass.

Keeping a clean edge is the tricky part. Fixera Services can reseal your shower or bathtub in under an hour, with crisp lines.

These issues are usually handled as a mix of small repairs and apartment installations depending on wall type and fittings. If these issues keep coming back, you can also explore our installation services or home repair services.

Bonus: other quick wins for old flats

  • Loose door handles or locks: tighten or replace the set screws.
  • Squeaky doors: a drop of silicone spray on hinges (avoid oil).
  • Wobbly furniture: old floors aren’t always level, shim or adjust.

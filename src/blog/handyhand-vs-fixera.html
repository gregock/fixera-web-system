Handyhand vs. Fixera Services – Which one really works better?

16 October 2025 • Read time: 6 minutes

Handyhand.dk is a popular Danish marketplace where anyone can post tasks and receive quotes from freelancers. Fixera Services is my direct handyman service: you deal with me (Greg), not a platform, and I handle the work personally. Both options can solve household jobs, but they perform differently depending on what you need.

I keep an eye on Handyhand rates because many customers come to me after difficult experiences. Below is a transparent comparison so you can decide which model protects your budget and your nerves.

Quick comparison

Fixera Services vs. Handyhand at a glance
Criteria Handyhand Marketplace Fixera Services
Pricing transparency Hourly rates vary by helper (+ platform fee). Final cost depends on negotiation. Fixed quote upfront (labour + materials) with clear scope and warranty.
Vetting & insurance Light vetting. Insurance depends on the individual helper; often none. Business liability insurance professional service (CVR 43921746) with invoices for tax and warranty.
Ideal types of tasks Simple chores, moving boxes, assembling IKEA furniture, cleaning. Repairs, installations, diagnostics, cabinetry, real-estate handovers, safety or compliance-sensitive work.
Support when something fails Handled through the platform. Resolution can take time and is not guaranteed. You speak directly with me. I fix the issue and document corrective work.

Where Handyhand shines

  • Simple labour: If you need someone to move boxes, shovel snow, or help with a short-term chore, the marketplace can be cost-effective.
  • Very tight budgets: Prices can be low if you accept slower response times or limited guarantees.
  • Fully DIY tasks: When you supervise everything and quality control is easy, the risk is lower.

Where Fixera Services is safer

Many clients contact me after a marketplace attempt went wrong. These are the situations where a specialised handyman saves the day:

  1. Repairs with hidden damage. Water leaks, rot, misaligned doors and electrical issues rarely follow a script. A project-based quote prevents runaway hourly billing and ensures the root cause is fixed.
  2. Documentation and invoices. Landlords, insurance companies and home buyers often request proof of work. With Fixera Services you receive a registered invoice and a written summary of the repair.
  3. Liability. If a handyman breaks something or leaves a job unsafe, you need insurance to cover it. Fixera Services carries full liability coverage so you are not left paying twice.

Cost example: door frame restoration

A recent client posted a rotted door frame on Handyhand. The quotes ranged between DKK 4,000 and DKK 6,000, plus materials, and nobody guaranteed the frame would close properly afterwards.

My Fixera Services quote was DKK 4,800 including materials, moisture treatment, a custom insert, sanding, finishing and a written handover. Final invoice: DKK 4,800, no surprises. The repaired door is now part of the project gallery.

How to decide quickly

Choose Handyhand if…

  • The task is light, repetitive, or you can re-check quality yourself.
  • You are comfortable coordinating several helpers and handling disputes.
  • Documentation and warranties are not important.

Choose Fixera Services if…

  • The job involves repairs, diagnostics, or building regulations.
  • You want a fixed price, insurance, and a single point of responsibility.
  • You need the work completed quickly with pro-grade tools and materials.

Bottom line

Marketplaces like Handyhand are great for light chores. When a project affects safety, resale value, or requires craftsmanship, you are better off with a vetted professional.

If you are unsure, send me a couple of photos on WhatsApp. I will tell you honestly if the task fits Fixera Services or if a cheaper marketplace route is fine.

For recurring landlord needs, I usually recommend direct support on furniture assembly and small repairs rather than ad-hoc marketplace coordination.

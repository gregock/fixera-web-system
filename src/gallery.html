About this gallery

At Fixera, results come from solid preparation, the right materials, and tidy finishing.

This gallery shows real work completed across Copenhagen homes, from quick fixes to full room refreshes. You will see clean drilling in concrete and tiles, precise furniture installs, and repairs that extend the life of everyday spaces. I bring professional tools and common supplies, document progress with photos, and leave everything neat before I go. Moving by cargo bike keeps travel fast and eco friendly across the city.

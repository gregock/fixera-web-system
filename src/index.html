FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Short, helpful answers to common questions.

  • How much does a handyman cost in Copenhagen?

    Most small handyman jobs start from 500–900 DKK for labor, depending on the task. You receive a clear estimate before the job starts, and parts or materials are quoted separately if needed.

  • What jobs can a handyman help with?

    Typical handyman jobs include installing lamps, mounting shelves or TVs, fixing doors, replacing faucets, resealing bathrooms, and other small repairs or installations around the home.

  • Which areas do you serve?

    Fixera provides handyman services across Copenhagen, Frederiksberg, Hellerup, Gladsaxe, Gentofte and Søborg, with fast response for nearby districts and apartment areas across the city.

  • How quickly can I book a handyman?

    Many small jobs can be scheduled within 24–48 hours. Sending photos of the job helps provide a faster quote and schedule.

  • Do you assemble IKEA furniture in Copenhagen?

    Yes. Professional IKEA furniture assembly is available for wardrobes, beds, shelving systems and other flat‑pack furniture, with clear labor pricing before work begins.

Countertop Replacement in Copenhagen

Kitchen countertop replacement in Copenhagen, moving from a worn sink-side surface and removal work to a cleaner newly fitted result.

Copenhagen
Countertop replacement Kitchen improvement Sink cutout Repair-first Kitchen carpentry

Story

This kitchen worktop had reached the point where the sink-side surface looked tired, stained and difficult to keep looking clean. The job was to remove the old countertop, prepare the area properly and fit a cleaner replacement so the kitchen would feel more usable again in everyday use.

Once the old top was lifted away, the exposed edge around the sink opening and cabinet run could be checked and cleaned up before the new piece went in. The replacement work focused on the countertop itself and the practical fitting around the existing kitchen layout, not on claiming separate plumbing or electrical scope beyond what the photos actually show.

The final result gave the kitchen a much fresher working surface and a neater finish around the sink area. This is the kind of repair-led improvement and practical carpentry work Northstar handles in Copenhagen homes.

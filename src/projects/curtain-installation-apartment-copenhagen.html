Story

This page brings together recent curtain installation examples from Copenhagen apartments. The visuals show typical stages from track preparation and bracket details to fabric adjustment and finished room results. They represent recent jobs rather than one exact photographed installation from start to finish.

In apartment curtain work, the practical difference usually comes from alignment and finish rather than the rail alone. The track needs to sit neatly against the opening, corners and returns need to look deliberate, and the curtain drop has to finish at the right height once the fabric settles naturally.

Across recent jobs, openings are checked first, rails are fixed so the run stays neat, and curtains are adjusted where needed so they glide cleanly and sit better in daily use. Small finishing decisions such as bracket placement and fabric length often make the difference between a temporary-looking setup and a room that feels complete.

This is the kind of curtain installation and apartment fitting work Northstar handles in everyday Copenhagen homes. If your tracks or curtains are already ready, you can also send photos for a quote.

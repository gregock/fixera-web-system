Custom Corner Curtain Rail Installation

Custom ceiling-mounted corner rail installation with tailored curtain hemming and exact floor clearance for an apartment in Nørrebro / Nordvest.

Nørrebro · Nordvest
Corner curtain rail Custom rail fitting Tailored hemming Ceiling-mounted Nørrebro

Story

The apartment needed a custom curtain rail solution that could run neatly through a corner while keeping a clean line at the ceiling. The curtains also needed tailored hemming so the final drop would clear the floor exactly instead of bunching.

I measured the room, fitted a ceiling-mounted corner rail layout and adjusted the curtain length to suit the finished run. The goal was a precise custom setup that looked calm, moved smoothly and avoided the compromises of a more generic off-the-shelf arrangement.

The finished result gave the client better privacy, cleaner light control and a more deliberate built-in look in the room. It was completed for an apartment in Nørrebro / Nordvest.

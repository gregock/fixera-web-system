Technical considerations for curtain installation

Proper curtain installation depends on wall type, bracket spacing, track length and the weight of the curtain or blind. These details are checked before drilling so the final setup hangs straight and runs smoothly.

  • Concrete, brick and gypsum walls require different drill bits, plugs and screw sizes.
  • Longer tracks and ceiling-mounted fittings need careful spacing so the rail stays level.
  • Bracket height and wall clearance affect how the curtain falls and opens in daily use.
  • Photos of the window, wall area and curtain or blind type usually help confirm the scope before the visit.

End of Tenancy Fixes

Fixera provides targeted end-of-tenancy repair services in Copenhagen for apartments that need to be handed back in good condition. Moving out often reveals holes, surface damage, loose fittings, and wear that landlords or agents notice during inspection.

The work focuses on resolving visible defects with proper execution: flush-filled holes, aligned hardware, and surfaces prepared to match existing finishes. This ensures the apartment meets expected handover condition without unnecessary overwork.

What this service covers

End-of-tenancy work focuses on repair, adjustment, and visible finish issues that are often flagged during handover inspections. The goal is to return the apartment in a more complete condition without turning the visit into a full renovation project.

  • Flush filling, sanding, and surface leveling for rawlplug and screw holes.
  • Re-alignment and tightening of cabinet hinges, doors, and loose hardware.
  • Surface preparation and localized touch-ups aligned with existing wall finish (paint code required).
  • Remediation of visible wear before landlord or agency inspection.

This service does not include full-room painting or floor sanding, end-of-tenancy deep cleaning, certified electrical or plumbing alterations, or major structural renovation work.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about end-of-tenancy repair work, handover condition, and what this service does or does not cover.

  • Is this service suitable for moving out?

    Yes. The work is specifically aligned with what landlords and administrators check during Danish move-out inspections (fraflytningssyn), including visible wall damage, hardware condition, and overall finish.

  • Do you coordinate with landlords or agencies?

    Work is executed based on your instructions. You can share your agency checklist or move-in report (indflytningsrapport) in advance to ensure the work matches the expected handover standard.

  • Can this replace professional move-out cleaning?

    No. This is a repair and adjustment service only. It should be scheduled before your final move-out cleaning so the apartment is ready for inspection.

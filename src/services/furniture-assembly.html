Typical furniture assembly jobs

Typical jobs focus on room-ready assembly where furniture is built, adjusted and safely placed for daily use.

  • Wardrobe and closet assembly with door alignment.
  • Bed and bedroom furniture assembly with stable leveling.
  • Shelving and storage systems assembled and anchored safely.

What’s included

  • Flat-pack assembly (IKEA and similar brands).
  • Leveling and adjustments so doors and drawers work properly.
  • Wall anchoring for tall or heavy units when required for safety.
  • Clean setup and packaging disposal.

What’s not included

  • Custom-built or made-to-measure furniture.
  • Structural carpentry or wood construction.
  • Electrical or plumbing work.

FAQ

  • Do you bring tools and fixings?

    Yes. I bring pro tools and common fixings. If special anchors are needed, I’ll confirm before purchasing.

  • Can you assemble and place in the room?

    Yes. I assemble, place, level and secure the unit if needed for safety.

  • What about walls in concrete or brick?

    I carry drill bits and anchors for concrete/brick/gypsum. Anchoring is included when it’s part of the safe install.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about installation work, wall types, fixings and how quotes are handled.

  • Do you install lamps on concrete or brick walls?

    Yes. I drill into concrete or brick using the proper plugs, screws and drill bits for a safe and tidy lamp installation.

  • Can you mount curtain rails and blinds?

    Yes. I measure, drill and mount curtain rails or blinds securely so they sit level and move properly.

  • Do you install shelves and wall-mounted fixtures?

    Yes. I install shelves, mirrors, hooks and similar wall-mounted fixtures with attention to alignment, anchoring and wall type.

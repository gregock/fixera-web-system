Technical considerations for lamp installation

Proper lamp installation depends on the ceiling or wall material, fixture weight, mounting point and whether the existing electrical connection can be reused without new wiring. These details are checked before drilling or fitting the lamp.

  • Concrete, brick and gypsum surfaces require different anchors, screws and drilling methods.
  • Pendant lamps, ceiling lights and wall lamps need different positioning, height and alignment checks.
  • Existing fixture boxes, cable exits and ceiling roses affect how cleanly a lamp can be mounted.
  • New electrical wiring, new circuits and hidden cable routing are not included in this service.

Photos of the lamp, mounting point, ceiling or wall surface and existing cable outlet usually help confirm the scope, fixing method and expected time before the visit.

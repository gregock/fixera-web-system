Fixera provides practical office maintenance and business support in Copenhagen for small workplaces that need furniture, shelves, screens, meeting rooms, cable management, Wi-Fi/router/printer setup and light workflow improvements in one visit.

The work is grounded in operations: keep the workplace functional, organized and easy to use without turning it into a separate IT consultancy.

How it works

Small businesses usually need a practical visit that combines physical setup and light technical support.

  • Share photos, room notes or a short list of what needs attention.
  • Describe what needs to be assembled, mounted or connected.
  • Fixera confirms scope and timing in plain terms before the visit.
  • The visit ends with a tidy setup and a short update on what was completed.

Technology support, kept practical

Technology support is part of keeping the workplace operational, not a separate enterprise IT offer.

Operational details

A few practical points are usually worth clarifying before the job starts.

  • Who is this for? Small businesses, offices, shops, cafes, studios, clinics and other commercial workplaces that need practical support.
  • Can one visit cover both furniture and tech? Yes. The visit can combine assembly, mounting, connection work and simple operational setup where it makes sense.
  • What is not included? No enterprise IT consulting, no server administration, and no major electrical or network build-out work.
  • How do updates work? After the visit you get a short update with what was completed, what was not touched, and whether anything needs follow-up.

Northstar provides technical home repair services in Copenhagen for hardware and surfaces that require proper execution. Whether a door binds against its frame, a cabinet hinge has torn out, or a wall needs flush patching, the focus is on restoring mechanical stability and visual alignment.

The service targets specific residential defects such as failed drywall anchors, sticking locks, misaligned joinery, and localized wear. These are resolved with the correct fillers, fixings, and adjustments rather than unnecessary replacement.

Typical repair jobs

Common repair work includes the types of issues that affect daily use and require proper adjustment or correction:

  • Planing sticking doors and re-aligning sagging cabinet hinges.
  • Extracting failed wall anchors and flush-filling drywall damage.
  • Re-securing loose handles, window locks, strikes, and architectural hardware.
  • Replacing broken drawer runners and stabilizing wobbly fixtures.

What this service covers

Repairs are focused on practical correction work that restores function, alignment, and everyday usability without turning the visit into a larger renovation job.

  • Adjustment of doors, hinges, strikes, and components that no longer close or sit correctly.
  • Replacement or correction of handles, knobs, and small fittings that affect daily use.
  • Wall repairs for minor holes, anchors, cracks, and visible wear that need proper preparation.
  • Repair-first assessment of small defects before recommending unnecessary replacement work.

This service does not include plumbing work involving pipes or drains, electrical rewiring, furniture assembly, or major structural renovations outside practical handyman scope.

Typical pricing

From 600 DKK depending on scope, parts, and the number of issues grouped into the same visit. Most straightforward repairs take 1–2 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about household repairs, minor corrections, and what this service does or does not cover.

  • Can you trim and align a sticking door?

    Yes. I can plane or trim doors, adjust hinges and strikes, and correct the alignment so the door closes more smoothly.

  • Do you mount shelves, hooks and mirrors?

    Yes. Where the task is still a straightforward repair or small fixing job, I can mount shelves, hooks, and mirrors using the correct plugs and screws for the wall type.

  • What if parts are missing or broken?

    I can suggest compatible replacements or purchase agreed parts and add the receipt to the quote, as long as the scope remains within practical handyman repair work.

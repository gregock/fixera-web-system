Skirting boards installation and repair in Copenhagen

We install, adjust and repair skirting boards for a clean, finished result. Whether you're working on a renovation, replacing damaged sections or fixing poorly fitted boards, we focus on precise corners, straight lines and tight finishes.

Get a quote WhatsApp

We install, adjust and repair skirting boards across Copenhagen with a focus on clean lines and durable fitting quality.

We measure, cut and fit each section carefully to ensure tight joints and clean alignment. Depending on the surface, we use appropriate fixing methods and finish edges so the result is ready for painting or final detailing.

Typical skirting board jobs

Typical situations include:

  • After new flooring installation
  • Renovation or refurbishment work
  • Damaged skirting from moisture or impact
  • Poorly installed existing boards
  • Visible gaps or uneven finishes

What’s included

  • Installation of new skirting boards
  • Cutting and fitting internal and external corners
  • Adjusting to uneven walls and floors
  • Fixing loose or damaged boards
  • Replacing sections of existing skirting
  • Preparing surfaces for paint-ready finish

What’s not included

  • Full apartment painting or decorating.
  • Flooring installation or sanding.
  • Structural carpentry or major renovation work.
  • Exterior trim or outdoor timber work.
  • Large multi-trade renovation coordination.

FAQ

  • Do you install new skirting boards?

    Yes. We install new skirting boards, replace damaged sections and adjust existing boards for a cleaner finish.

  • Can you fix loose or damaged skirting boards?

    Yes. We can refit loose boards, replace damaged sections and improve uneven joints or visible gaps where possible.

  • Do you also paint the skirting boards?

    We prepare the boards for a paint-ready finish, but full apartment painting is not included in this service.

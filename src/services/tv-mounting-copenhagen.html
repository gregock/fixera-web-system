Technical considerations for TV wall mounting

Proper TV wall mounting depends on wall type, bracket compatibility, screen size and the way the load is transferred into the wall. These details are checked before drilling so the installation can be planned safely.

  • Concrete, brick and gypsum walls require different drill bits, anchors and fixing methods.
  • The TV weight, VESA pattern and bracket type are checked before the wall position is marked.
  • Viewing height, furniture placement and room layout are considered before final drilling.
  • Cable routing is kept practical and tidy, but in-wall cable routing and electrical rewiring are not included.

Photos of the TV, bracket, wall and nearby power outlet usually allow the scope and fixing approach to be confirmed before the visit.
